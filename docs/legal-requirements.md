# Norwegian HMS Legal Requirements for Housing Associations (Borettslag and Sameier)

> Researched 2026-04-15. Sources include lovdata.no, DSB, DiBK, and practitioner guides from USBL, OBOS, Styrelederne, Huseierne, and others. Links at bottom.

## Legal Framework

The **board (styret)** is the responsible party for nearly all obligations — acting as "virksomhetsleder" (enterprise leader) and "eier" (owner) in the eyes of safety legislation.

Key regulations:
- **Internkontrollforskriften** (FOR-1996-12-06-1127) — foundational HMS regulation
- **Forskrift om brannforebygging** (FOR-2015-12-17-1710) — fire safety
- **Brann- og eksplosjonsvernloven** (LOV-2002-06-14-20) — fire/explosion prevention
- **Borettslagsloven** (LOV-2003-06-06-39) — housing cooperative act
- **Eierseksjonsloven** (LOV-2017-06-16-65) — owner-section act
- **Plan- og bygningsloven** § 29-9 + **TEK17** kapittel 16 — elevator safety
- **Byggherreforskriften** (FOR-2009-08-03-1028) — construction/rehabilitation projects
- **Strålevernforskriften** — radon (mandatory for rental units)
- Standards: NS-EN 1176 (playgrounds), NEK EN 50172 / NS-EN 1838 (emergency lighting), NS 3960 (fire alarm), NS-EN 12845 / FG-930:1 (sprinkler)

## Category 1: Internkontroll (HMS System) — Internkontrollforskriften §§ 4–5

### 1.1 Establish and Maintain a Written HMS System
- **Frequency**: Ongoing; formal review minimum annually
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 4 (plikt til internkontroll), § 5 (innhold og dokumentasjon)
- **Responsible**: Styreleder on behalf of the board; must involve residents
- **Must document**: Yes — § 5 mandates written documentation of: (a) HMS goals, (b) organisational structure and responsibility allocation, (c) risk assessment and mitigation plans, (d) deviation/correction procedures, (e) systematic monitoring/review

### 1.2 Annual HMS System Review
- **Frequency**: Minimum once per year (typically tied to annual general meeting cycle)
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5, item 8
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — written review record required

### 1.3 Risk Assessment and Hazard Mapping
- **Task**: Identify hazards in all common areas and installations, assess likelihood and consequences, implement mitigating measures. Covers: electrical systems, fire safety, playgrounds, ventilation, stairwells, outdoor areas
- **Frequency**: Ongoing; formally updated at each annual review and after any incident
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5, item 6
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — written risk register required

### 1.4 Deviation Reporting and Corrective Action
- **Task**: Operate a system to detect, log, correct, and prevent recurrence of deviations from HMS requirements. Residents must be able to report faults
- **Frequency**: Continuous / as incidents arise
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5, item 7
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — deviation log required

## Category 2: Fire Safety — Forskrift om brannforebygging §§ 4–10

### 2.1 Smoke Detector Testing
- **Task**: Test function of all smoke detectors in common areas. Replace batteries. Ensure detectors are not older than 10 years
- **Frequency**: Monthly (recommended); annually minimum (board for common areas). Battery replacement: annually. Device replacement: every 10 years
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 7; Brann- og eksplosjonsvernloven § 6
- **Responsible**: Board (common areas); residents (own unit). Borettslag: board has collective responsibility for all units
- **Must document**: Yes — service log

### 2.2 Fire Extinguisher Inspection and Service
- **Task**: Visual inspection of all extinguishers (pressure gauge, hose, seal, powder clumping). Professional service by approved personnel
- **Frequency**: Visual self-check: every 6 months (powder/CO₂). Professional inspection: annually. Professional service: every 5 years (powder); every 10 years (CO₂)
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 7; § 5
- **Responsible**: Board for common areas; certified professional for 5/10-year service
- **Must document**: Yes — service labels on equipment; written service report

### 2.3 Fire Hose Inspection (Brannslangetrommler)
- **Frequency**: Annually by certified personnel; periodic checks per manufacturer instructions
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 5, § 7; Brann- og eksplosjonsvernloven § 6
- **Responsible**: Board / certified fire safety company
- **Must document**: Yes — service report

### 2.4 Fire Alarm System Annual Service
- **Task**: Annual inspection and function test. Verify system still meets requirements after any building changes. Verify automatic alarm transmission if required
- **Frequency**: Annually — must be performed by a certified company (FG-approved or equivalent)
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 5; NS 3960; Brann- og eksplosjonsvernloven § 6
- **Responsible**: Board responsible; certified fire alarm company performs the work
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection report; registered in relevant database

### 2.5 Sprinkler System Inspection and Service
- **Task**: Annual comprehensive inspection by FG-approved inspector. Additional maintenance of valves and equipment every 6 months – 3 years depending on components
- **Frequency**: Annual inspection (FG-approved company); maintenance intervals: 6 months – 3 years depending on component
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 5; NS-EN 12845; FG-930:1
- **Responsible**: Board; FG-approved inspector for annual inspection
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection report must be registered in FG-kontroll database

### 2.6 Emergency and Evacuation Lighting Inspection (Nødlys/Ledelys)
- **Task**: Inspect all emergency lighting and evacuation marking lights in stairwells and common areas. Verify automatic activation on power failure
- **Frequency**: Annually
- **Legal basis**: TEK17; NEK EN 50172; NS-EN 1838; Internkontrollforskriften
- **Responsible**: Board; certified electrical/fire safety company performs inspection
- **Must document**: Yes — owner must document that emergency lighting functions as intended

### 2.7 Evacuation Route Inspection (Rømningsveier)
- **Task**: Inspect all evacuation routes, stairwells, and emergency exits — unobstructed, correctly signed, and accessible
- **Frequency**: Minimum twice per year; ongoing monitoring
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 5, § 9; Internkontrollforskriften § 5
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection log

### 2.8 Fire Safety Walkthrough (Brannvernrunde)
- **Task**: Systematic walkthrough of all common areas and stairwells. Check fire compartmentalisation (brannceller), fire doors close properly, no improper storage in escape routes
- **Frequency**: Minimum twice per year; many organisations do quarterly
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging §§ 5, 9, 10; Internkontrollforskriften
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — written round report

### 2.9 Resident Fire Safety Information
- **Task**: Distribute fire safety instructions; inform residents about equipment, evacuation routes, and individual responsibilities
- **Frequency**: Annually (minimum); after any new resident moves in
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging § 4; Brann- og eksplosjonsvernloven § 6
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — record of distribution

### 2.10 Chimney Sweeping and Heating System Inspection
- **Task**: Ensure chimneys are swept and heating systems inspected. **Note: this is a municipal duty** — municipality arranges and invoices the owner. Board must provide access and report new fireplaces
- **Frequency**: As needed per Forskrift om brannforebygging § 17; typically every 4 years in practice; immediately after any chimney fire
- **Legal basis**: Forskrift om brannforebygging §§ 6, 17
- **Responsible**: Municipality arranges; board provides access and reports new fireplaces
- **Must document**: Municipality documents; owner keeps records of notifications

## Category 3: Electrical Installations — Internkontrollforskriften + El-tilsynslova

### 3.1 Electrical Installation Inspection (Elektrokontroll)
- **Task**: Comprehensive inspection of all electrical installations in common areas and outdoor areas (stairwells, basement, parking, EV chargers, exterior lighting). Per NEK 405 standard
- **Frequency**: Every 5 years (minimum)
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5; El-tilsynslova; DLE oversight
- **Responsible**: Board; inspection must be performed by a qualified electrician certified per NEK 405
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection report specifying what is in order and what must be remedied

## Category 4: Elevators and Lifting Devices — TEK17 Kapittel 16 / Plan- og bygningsloven § 29-9

### 4.1 Mandatory Periodic Safety Inspection
- **Task**: Independent safety inspection by an accredited safety control body (SKO). Distinct from routine service. Findings reported to NIREG and owner
- **Frequency**: Minimum every 2 years (biennial). Intensive-use buildings may require more frequent inspections per TEK17 Table 1 & 2
- **Legal basis**: Plan- og bygningsloven § 29-9; TEK17 §§ 16-1, 16-2; NS-INSTA 500-2:2017
- **Responsible**: Board arranges and pays; only accredited SKO may perform (e.g. Norsk Heiskontroll, Kiwa)
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection report; logged in NIREG; logbook maintained on-site

### 4.2 Elevator Daily Supervision and Routine Service
- **Task**: Daily supervision to check for external damage and proper function; routine maintenance per NS-EN 13015. Halt operation immediately if safety risk identified
- **Frequency**: Daily supervision (board); routine service per service agreement (typically monthly or quarterly)
- **Legal basis**: TEK17 § 16-1; NS-EN 13015
- **Responsible**: Board (daily supervision); elevator service company for routine maintenance
- **Must document**: Yes — logbook required for all repairs and maintenance

### 4.3 Elevator Accident/Incident Reporting
- **Task**: Immediately report accidents or serious incidents to municipality and NIREG. Cease operation until safety control authority approves resumption
- **Frequency**: As events occur
- **Legal basis**: TEK17 § 16-1
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — mandatory reporting

## Category 5: Playgrounds — Produktkontrolloven + NS-EN 1176

### 5.1 Routine Visual Inspection
- **Task**: Visual check for obvious hazards: broken parts, sharp edges, dangerous debris, entrapment risks, vandalism
- **Frequency**: Weekly to monthly (depending on use intensity)
- **Legal basis**: Produktkontrolloven; NS-EN 1176; Internkontrollforskriften § 5
- **Responsible**: Board or designated caretaker
- **Must document**: Yes — inspection log

### 5.2 Functional (Operational) Inspection
- **Task**: Detailed inspection of wear on moving parts (swings, slides, bearings), fasteners, corrosion, fall surface depth
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Legal basis**: NS-EN 1176; Internkontrollforskriften
- **Responsible**: Board or trained person
- **Must document**: Yes

### 5.3 Annual Main Inspection (Hovedkontroll)
- **Task**: Comprehensive structural integrity assessment by a certified playground inspector. Includes fall surfaces, anchoring, structural strength, NS-EN 1176 compliance. Results in a formal report with mandatory remediation list
- **Frequency**: Annually
- **Legal basis**: NS-EN 1176; Produktkontrolloven; Internkontrollforskriften
- **Responsible**: Board arranges; must be performed by a certified playground inspector (sertifisert lekeplasskontrollør)
- **Must document**: Yes — detailed written inspection report

## Category 6: Ventilation Systems

### 6.1 Annual Filter Replacement and Service
- **Frequency**: Annually
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5; TEK17 requirements for indoor air quality
- **Responsible**: Board / ventilation service company
- **Must document**: Yes — service report

### 6.2 Ventilation Duct Cleaning (Kanalrens)
- **Task**: Professional cleaning of all ventilation ducts to remove dust, bacteria, and contaminants
- **Frequency**: Every 5–7 years (5 years widely used; SINTEF/Byggforsk guidance: every 7 years for normal residential). **Note: best practice / industry standard rather than a specific statutory interval**
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5 (systematic maintenance); TEK17 indoor air quality
- **Responsible**: Board / certified ventilation company
- **Must document**: Yes — service report

---

## Category 7: Plumbing and Water Systems

### 7.1 Stop Valve Inspection (Stoppekran)
- **Task**: Inspect and test main stop valves and all unit-level stop valves for function. Ensure valves are accessible and clearly marked
- **Frequency**: Annually
- **Legal basis**: Internkontrollforskriften § 5; TEK17 § 15-6
- **Responsible**: Board (common pipes and risers); residents (own unit valves)
- **Must document**: Yes — maintenance log

---

## Category 8: Radon — Strålevernforskriften

### 8.1 Radon Measurement
- **Task**: Measure radon concentration in ground floor and lower floors. Mandatory for any unit rented out. Measurements must take place 15 October – 15 April over minimum 2 months
- **Frequency**: **Mandatory for rental units** — every 5–10 years or after remediation. Always after major renovation. Action required if >100 Bq/m³; must stay below 200 Bq/m³
- **Legal basis**: Strålevernforskriften (mandatory for rental units); DSA guidelines; Borettslagsloven § 5-17 / Eierseksjonsloven § 40
- **Responsible**: Board (as owner/landlord for leased units and common responsibility)
- **Must document**: Yes — measurement report from accredited laboratory

---

## Category 9: Construction and Rehabilitation Projects — Byggherreforskriften

### 9.1 SHA Plan for Rehabilitation/Construction Projects
- **Task**: Before commencing any significant construction, rehabilitation, or maintenance project (facade work, roof replacement, pipe rehabilitation, window replacement, corridor painting), prepare a written SHA plan (Sikkerhet, Helse og Arbeidsmiljø). The board acts as "byggherre" and must ensure contractor compliance
- **Frequency**: Before every qualifying project (triggered by project initiation, not a recurring interval)
- **Legal basis**: Byggherreforskriften §§ 6, 8
- **Responsible**: Board (as byggherre); must appoint a coordinating HSE representative for large projects
- **Must document**: Yes — written SHA plan required before work begins

---

## Category 10: Governance — Borettslagsloven / Eierseksjonsloven

### 10.1 Annual General Meeting
- **Task**: Hold ordinary generalforsamling (borettslag) or årsmøte (sameie). Agenda must include: approval of annual accounts and annual report, election of board members, handling of any matters specified by law or statutes
- **Frequency**: Annually — must be held by end of June each year
- **Legal basis**: Borettslagsloven § 7-4; Eierseksjonsloven § 41
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — minutes (protokoll) required

### 10.2 Annual Accounts and Annual Report
- **Task**: Prepare and present annual accounts (årsregnskap) and annual report (årsberetning) to the general assembly
- **Frequency**: Annually — within 6 months of fiscal year end
- **Legal basis**: Borettslagsloven § 10-1; Eierseksjonsloven § 44; Regnskapsloven
- **Responsible**: Board; typically reviewed or audited by accountant
- **Must document**: Yes

### 10.3 Maintenance Planning (Vedlikeholdsplan)
- **Task**: Maintain a rolling maintenance plan covering the building's condition and planned future maintenance/capital expenditure
- **Frequency**: Ongoing; formally reviewed and updated annually at board level
- **Legal basis**: Borettslagsloven § 5-17; Eierseksjonsloven § 40
- **Responsible**: Board
- **Must document**: Yes — written plan strongly recommended

## Consolidated Frequency Summary

| Frequency | Task | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **Daily** | Elevator supervision (visual check, function) | TEK17 § 16-1 |
| **Monthly** | Smoke detector test (resident); playground visual check | Brannforebyggingsforskriften § 7; NS-EN 1176 |
| **Quarterly** | Playground functional inspection; fire extinguisher self-check (powder) | NS-EN 1176; Internkontrollforskriften |
| **Twice yearly** | Evacuation route inspection; fire safety walkthrough (brannvernrunde) | Brannforebyggingsforskriften §§ 5, 9; Internkontrollforskriften |
| **Annually** | HMS system review; risk assessment update; annual general meeting; annual accounts; smoke detector test + battery (board); fire alarm system service; emergency lighting inspection; sprinkler inspection; playground main inspection; ventilation filter service; stop valve check; resident fire safety information | Multiple — see above |
| **Every 2 years** | Elevator mandatory safety inspection (biennial minimum) | TEK17 § 16-1; Plan- og bygningsloven § 29-9 |
| **Every 5 years** | Electrical installation inspection (elektrokontroll); fire extinguisher professional service (powder); radon re-measurement (post-remediation) | Internkontrollforskriften; El-tilsynslova |
| **Every 5–7 years** | Ventilation duct cleaning (kanalrens) | Internkontrollforskriften + SINTEF guidance |
| **Every 10 years** | Fire extinguisher service (CO₂); smoke detector replacement (device age limit); radon re-measurement (stable low levels) | Brannforebyggingsforskriften § 7 |
| **Per project** | SHA plan for any rehabilitation/construction project | Byggherreforskriften |

## Documentation Requirements

All documentation should be:
- Written (electronic format acceptable)
- Retained and accessible for supervisory authorities
- Transferred completely to incoming board members at board changeovers
- Proportionate to building complexity and risk level (Internkontrollforskriften § 5)

Supervisory authorities:
- **DSB** — Direktoratet for samfunnssikkerhet og beredskap (fire)
- **DLE/Eltilsynet** — electrical installations
- **Kommunen** — building and elevator
- **Arbeidstilsynet** — worker safety aspects
